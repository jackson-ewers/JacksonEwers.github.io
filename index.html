NBA Game Simulation

In this project, we developed a sophisticated predictive model for NBA game outcomes utilizing probabilistic simulations. By scraping comprehensive NBA player and team data from the 2023-2024 season via Basketball Reference, we constructed a robust framework capable of forecasting the winner across 1,000 simulated matchups of any selected teams. The model meticulously simulates the point production of both the starting five and the top eight role players from each team. Key factors influencing these predictions include individual shooting performance, playing time data, and team defensive ratings. This multifaceted approach ensures a nuanced and accurate projection of game results.
